- 경쟁사: Freeport-McMoRan(FCX, Cerro Verde 운영사), Newmont, Southern Copper, Fortuna Silver, Hochschild Mining, Pan American Silver.
- 본사: Lima, Peru. 섹터: Basic Materials / Other Precious Metals & Mining.
